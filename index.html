About Me

My name is Vaughn Miller, and welcome to my portfolio website.

I am a student at Iowa State University, working towards a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering. I am interested in microcontroller programming, sensor networks, and data acquisition, primarily in machines with tires and a driver. Vehicles are integral to both work and play, so effective and informative development and testing are crucial to making them safe, predictable, and sometimes fast. I would like to be part of a team that makes all of that possible with the help of computers. Outside of computers, I enjoy making music, listening to it, and being with the people I love.
